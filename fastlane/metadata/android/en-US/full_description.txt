<i>Zenith</i> is a smart focus assistant for Android designed to help you reduce digital distractions through proactive intervention based on Material Design 3 Expressive.

Zenith is fully free and open-source. You can find the source code and report bugs or suggest features at https://github.com/1372Slash/Zenith

<b>Features:</b>
- Simple, minimalist UI based on the latest Material 3 Expressive guidelines
- Smart Intervention System:
    - Shield Mode: Mindful pauses to prevent addictive scrolling
    - Delay App: Intentional time delays before opening protected apps
    - Goal Pursuit: Targets for your productive applications
- Comprehensive Tracking:
    - Session HUD: Floating overlay with real-time session countdown
    - Detailed Usage Stats: Visualize your daily and weekly digital habits
    - Smart Schedules: Automate your focus periods
- Built-in Mindfulness:
    - Emergency Use: Restricted access quota for critical moments
    - Privacy First: Operates locally with required permissions for core functionality
